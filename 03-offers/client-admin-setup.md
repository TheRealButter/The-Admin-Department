# Offer — Client Admin Setup

## Product name

Client Admin Setup

## One-line promise

Onboard clients properly without losing documents, forgetting steps, or chasing the same information manually.

## Who this is for

- agencies
- consultants
- accountants/bookkeepers
- service businesses
- legal/admin-heavy firms
- contractors with project onboarding
- property managers onboarding landlords/tenants

## What problem it fixes

New clients often start through scattered emails, WhatsApp messages, documents, deposits, agreements, folders, and internal notes.

The result:

- welcome messages are inconsistent
- required documents are not tracked properly
- agreements and payments delay delivery
- folders are created late or inconsistently
- staff do not know what is still missing
- clients get a messy first experience
- owners do not know who is ready to start

## What we install

- onboarding tracker
- client status pipeline
- required document checklist
- welcome message templates
- missing-document templates
- folder structure
- agreement/payment tracking
- internal handover checklist
- weekly onboarding report
- simple handover SOP

For ongoing support.

## What we do not do

- We do not replace legal review.
- We do not confirm payment unless connected to verified payment data.
- We do not make business decisions for the client.
- We do not send sensitive documents without human approval.
- We do not replace full project management software at MVP stage.
