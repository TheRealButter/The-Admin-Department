# Offer — Sales Admin Setup

## Product name

Sales Admin Setup

## One-line promise

Stop losing enquiries because replies, quotes, and follow-ups are scattered across WhatsApp, email, calls, and spreadsheets.

## Who this is for

- contractors
- plumbers
- electricians
- gate/garage/security installers
- cleaning companies
- repair businesses
- small agencies
- consultants
- real estate and rental teams

## What problem it fixes

Leads arrive from different places. Some are WhatsApp messages. Some are website forms. Some come through calls, DMs, emails, referrals, or walk-ins.

The result:

- serious enquiries are not replied to quickly
- quotes are sent but not followed up
- owners do not know which leads are open
- staff forget who needs the next action
- prospects go cold without a clear reason
- sales reports depend on memory

## What we install

- enquiry tracker
- lead/contact structure
- lead statuses
- urgency rules
- quote follow-up queue
- first-response rules
- lost/cold reason tracking
- message templates
- weekly sales admin report
- simple handover SOP

## What we do not do

- We do not guarantee sales.
- We do not replace the business owner’s sales judgment.
- We do not send autonomous messages without approval.
- We do not replace a full CRM at MVP stage.
- We do not manage paid ads or generate leads as part of this setup.
